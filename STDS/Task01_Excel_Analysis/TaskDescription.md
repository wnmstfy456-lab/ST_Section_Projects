
# Task 01: Excel Analysis

## Objective
To perform comprehensive data analysis on HR and food-related datasets using Microsoft Excel, extracting actionable insights and demonstrating proficiency in Excel's analytical capabilities.

## Scope
This task involves analyzing two distinct datasets:
1.  **HR Data.xlsx**: Focuses on human resources data, including employee demographics, performance metrics, and other relevant HR-related information.
2.  **تحليل خاص بالمواد الغذائية.xlsx**: Contains data related to food analysis, potentially covering sales, inventory, consumption, or supply chain aspects.

## Key Aspects
*   Data cleaning and preparation within Excel.
*   Application of Excel formulas and functions for statistical analysis.
*   Creation of pivot tables and charts for data summarization and visualization.
*   Identification of trends, patterns, and anomalies in both HR and food datasets.
*   Development of a clear and concise summary of findings from the analysis.
